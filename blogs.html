What Makes these Blog Different

Most writing blogs are built around the US publishing market. They reference USD pricing, American ISBNs, and platforms that do not always apply cleanly to Australian authors navigating AUD costs, GST obligations, Thorpe-Bowker registration, and the National Library's legal deposit requirements.

Every article on this blog is written with the Aussie author experience at the centre. You will not need to mentally convert advice or wonder whether it applies to you. It does because it was built for you.
